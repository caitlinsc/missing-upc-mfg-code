CSV UPC filter

Two tools in one. Filter: remove rows that already have a UPC. Fill from IMU: use a master IMU export to fill in missing UPCs by SKU.

1Upload CSV

The tool looks for a column named UPC, Barcode, or GTIN. Cells that are blank, 0, or N/A count as missing.

2Filter

3Fill missing UPCs from IMU

Upload a master IMU CSV. The tool will look up each row's SKU and fill in any missing UPC from IMU. SKUs not found in IMU (or with a blank IMU UPC) are kept in the output and highlighted in the preview.
